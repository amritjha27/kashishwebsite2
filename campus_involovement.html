MODEL UNITED NATIONS

I have been an active participant in the illustrious community of Model United Nations for a number of years. Being a part of MUN has been a truly enlightening experience for me, as it has granted me the opportunity to explore the intricacies of international diplomacy and develop a profound understanding of global affairs. I have had the privilege of representing various nations in a plethora of MUN conferences, where I have been able to simulate the United Nations, honing my oratory, research, and negotiation skills. Through these experiences, I have also gained an enhanced appreciation for diverse cultures, perspectives, and ways of thinking. Furthermore, I have learned to think critically, articulate my thoughts with clarity and find a common ground with others. Being a part of the MUN community has been an incredibly valuable and fulfilling experience for me and I look forward to continuing to be a part of it in the future.

DRAMA AND THEATER

"Oscar Wilde once said that theater is the greatest of all art forms, as it allows individuals to share a sense of humanity with one another. This sentiment was brought to life at Mayo College Girls' School during a four-day Dramatics Workshop on Improvisational Theater, led by the accomplished actor, Sir Gavin Noel Methalaka. Over the course of the Level-1 Introduction Course, participants learned to master the art of portraying characters through body language, building and simplifying storylines, and utilizing the concept of "Yes And" to accept and build upon given situations. The workshop not only helped participants develop their skills as actors, but also gave them the tools to navigate and build upon unexpected challenges on stage and in real life.".

ROBOTICS AND AUTOMATION

I was delighted to be chosen to take part in the Robotics Workshop at the Robotics Lab in Mayo College Girls' School from 7th to 8th September, 2022. We were privileged to learn from Mr. Sanjay Gupta, an IIT Delhi graduate, who briefed us on the fundamentals of Arduino Technology. Then, with the help of LED lights, Motors, Water Pumps, Temperature Detectors, Moisture Sensors, LDRs and more, we built projects. The best parts of this experience were the insightful conversations on the basics of Robotics and the practical experience with the Arduino kits which further solidified my concepts.
